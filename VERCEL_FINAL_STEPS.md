# Deploy to Vercel - Final Steps

Your GitHub repository is ready! Now let's deploy to Vercel and go LIVE! 🚀

## Step 1: Go to Vercel

Visit: https://vercel.com/dashboard

## Step 2: Import Your Project

1. Click "Add New..." → "Project"
2. Click "Import Git Repository"
3. Paste your GitHub URL: `https://github.com/rp72wfw8fm-del/Klick-website`
4. Click "Continue"

## Step 3: Configure Project

1. **Project Name:** `klick-website` (or any name you like)
2. **Framework Preset:** Select "Vite"
3. **Root Directory:** Leave as is (.)
4. Click "Deploy"

## Step 4: Wait for Deployment

Vercel will automatically:
- Build your website
- Optimize it
- Deploy it to their servers
- Give you a live URL

**This takes about 2-3 minutes!** ⏳

## Step 5: Get Your Live URL

After deployment, you'll see:
- **Production URL:** `https://klick-website.vercel.app` (or similar)
- This is your LIVE website! 🎉

## Step 6: Share Your Website!

Your website is now live! You can:
- ✅ Share the URL with anyone
- ✅ Post on social media
- ✅ Send to customers
- ✅ Add to your business card

## Optional: Connect Your Own Domain

If you have a domain (e.g., klick.app):
1. In Vercel dashboard, go to your project
2. Click "Settings" → "Domains"
3. Add your domain
4. Follow DNS setup instructions
